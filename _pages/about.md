I envision a future where AI can think, interact, and adapt as naturally as humans do. I aspire to be among the researchers who bridge today's technology to that future. To realize this vision, my research explores both the advancement and societal implications of AI.

I study how to enhance large language models' human-likeness by designing agents that model reasoning, emotions, and conversational dynamics. Drawing on psychology, I examine both positive and negative aspects of human cognition — such as empathy, motivation, and bias — to investigate how these traits can be reflected or controlled in AI systems. Ultimately, my goal is to help shape human-like AI that is not only intelligent, but also safe, fair, and deeply aware of people.
